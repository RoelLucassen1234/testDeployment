In dit hoofdstuk zal ik een calculatie uitvoeren om de RSA te encrypten en berekenen.

Hoofdstuk 1, creating public and private key

p en q moeten priemgetallen zijn. In dit geval ga ik p = 23 en q = 17 gebruiken.

n = pq
p = 23
q = 17
n = 17 * 23
n = 391
phi = (p-1) * (q-1)
phi = 16 * 22
phi = 352
1 < e < phi
1 < e < 352
e = 53 (e can be a random number that 1 < e < φ(n) and GCD(e, φ(n)) = 1. For this example we pick 53. 53 is a prime number and thus only divisible by 1 or itself.)
phi : e = 6,6 dus greatest common divider = 1
ed = 1 mod 352
d = e-1 mod 352
gcd(352, 53) ---> 34 = 352 - 6 * 53
gcd(53, 34) --> 19 = 53 - 1 * 34
gcd(34, 19) --> 15 = 34 - 1 * 19
gcd(19,15) --> 4 = 19 - 1 * 15
gcd(15, 4) --> 3 = 15 - 4 * 3
gcd(4, 3) --> 1 = 4 - 1 * 3

1 = 4-1 * 3
1 = 4 - 1 * (15 - 4 * 3) = 1 * 15 + 3 * 4 = 4 * 4 - 1 * 15
1 = 4 * (19 -1 * 15) - 1 * 15 = 4 * 19 - 4 * 15 - 1 * 15 = 4 * 19 - 5 * 15
1 = 4 * 19 - 5 * (34 - 1 * 19) = 4 * 19 - 5 * 34 + 5 * 19 = 9 * 19 - 5 * 34
1 = 9 * (53 - 1 * 34) - 5 * 34 = 9 * 53 - 9 * 34 - 5 * 34 = 9 * 53 - 14 * 34
1 = 9 * 53 - 14 * (352 - 6 * 53) = 9 * 53 - 14 * 352 + 84 * 53 = 93 * 53 - 14 * 352

1 = 93 * 53 - 14 * 352 (mod 352)
1 = 93 * 53 (mod 352)
a * inverse a = 1
d = 93

ed = 1 (mod phi)
93 * 53 (mod 352) = 1
